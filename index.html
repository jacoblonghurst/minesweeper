MineSweeper

How to Play

Overview

The object of Minesweeper is to locate all the mines as quickly as possible without uncovering any of them. If you uncover a mine, you lose the game.

How to Win

Once you've located a mine right click on the mine to place a flag. Once you've placed all flags and uncovered all the squares that are not mines, you win the game.

Game Start, Stop, Pause

To start a new game click Play. To stop a game click Stop. To pause a game click Pause. To save a current game for later click Save
